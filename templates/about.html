Hi, I'm Sarah, your local yogi!

I began my yoga practice at a time when I needed both physical and mental healing. In seeking more holistic practices and healthy habits, yoga became foundational for my well-being. As a former English teacher, I naturally fell into teaching others and interlacing themes told through movement and meditation.

I attained my RYT-200 through Higher Power Healing, developing a foundation in Hatha teaching practices. After moving from North Carolina to Michigan, I felt a deep desire to not only continue my practice, but to share it in a way that felt accessible, welcoming, and rooted in connection.

That's where my heart for community-based yoga began. I believe yoga doesn't have to live only inside a studio, it can exist in the everyday spaces where life is already happening. My goal is to bring yoga into the community, creating opportunities for people to gather, move, and breathe together in environments that feel familiar and inviting.

My classes are designed to be approachable, lighthearted, and meaningful. Whether you're brand new to yoga or returning to your mat, you'll find a space where you can show up as you are, without pressure or expectation.

At the end of the day, my hope is simple: to help you feel more at home in your body, more present in your life, and more connected to the community around you.
