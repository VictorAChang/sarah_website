Sarah Chang

Author

Children's Books

Sarah Chang, author of Henry Goes to the Zoo con su Abuelo, celebrates the joys of bilingualism, creating a character representation of a Spanish-English speaking family. This book can be found on the shelves of children in several states across the nation and three countries. Purchase your copy on Amazon.

Another children’s book, following the bilingual adventures of Henry, is currently in the works. Follow Sarah on Instagram to get updates on when you can buy the next book!

Additionally, Sarah offers author visitations for students of all ages! Students will get to hear her read her book, ask questions about the writing and design process, and participate in an activity to dive deeper into the many lessons presented in this book.

Fiction

In the works, Sarah is writing her first novel. In an engaging tale of a young woman who is faced with a ‘yes’ or ‘no’ option, the reader gets to embrace the journey of both answers, following this woman as she struggles with some of life’s most difficult moments, only to learn that her faith will be the only constant in any choice she makes.

Curriculum

For 10 years, Sarah taught high school and middle school English in both Michigan and North Carolina. Though she no longer spends long hours in the classroom, she continues to write lessons for English teachers, designing engaging activities for students to further their reading skills.

Click the Google Drive to link to public samples of Sarah’s ELA curriculum.

Additionally, Sarah writes Bible-based curriculum for churches to help students dive deeper into their understanding of the Bible.
