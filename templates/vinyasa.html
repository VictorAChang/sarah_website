Saturday morning vinyasa

When?

Every Saturday at 9am

Where?

Ever & Ollie in Downtown Flushing

Cost?

$12

Saturday Morning Vinyasa is a space to reset, reconnect, and begin your weekend with purpose.

Each session includes breathwork, fluid movement, and short pauses for grounding.

You can expect a supportive atmosphere where progress is personal, not performative.

Bring water, a yoga mat, and anything else that helps you feel comfortable.

Come as you are. Leave feeling stronger, clearer, and more centered.
