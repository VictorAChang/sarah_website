This playful yoga class is all about letting go, having fun, and not taking yourself too seriously. Set in a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere, we'll move through a lighthearted flow that weaves in mimosas. And yes, you might find yourself balancing with a glass in hand or toasting in a pose or two.

Expect laughter, connection, and feel-good movement designed for every level. Whether you're coming with friends or showing up solo, this class is your chance to unwind, try something new, and enjoy yoga in a completely approachable way.

What to expect when you arrive?

Pay online anytime before the class on OpenTable.

Enter through the front doors on East Main Street and roll out your yoga mat on the dining room floor. Your mimosa (included in the cost) will be served before class begins and used as a prop throughout the one-hour class.

As a courtesy, please arrive on time.

Stay after for some delicious food and get 10% off your meal!
