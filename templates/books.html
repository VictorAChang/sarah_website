Hi, I'm Sarah, author & illustrator!

My children's book, Henry Goes to the Zoo con su abuelo, celebrates the joys of bilingulism, creating a character representation of a Spanish-English speaking family. This book has been sold in multiple countries, including the U.S., Australia, Peru, and Colombia! Purchase yours right here!

Another children's book, following the bilingual adventures of Henry, is currently in the works. Follow Sarah on Instagram to get updates on when you can buy the next book!

Additionally, Sarah offers author visitations for students of all ages! Students will get to hear her read her book, ask questions about the writing and design process, and participate in an activity to dive deeper into the many lessons presented in this book.

Curriculum

With over a decade of experience teaching middle and high school English in Michigan and North Carolina, I have a love for developing curriculum for teenagers. I specialize in writing Bible-based curriculum for churches and ministries. My lessons are thoughtfully designed to help students not only understand the stories and teachings of Scripture but also connect them to real-life situations and personal spiritual growth. With a focus on age-appropriate engagement and theological depth, I create studies that encourage meaningful discussion, reflection, and application.

Please contact me if you are interested in a unique curriculum for your students!
